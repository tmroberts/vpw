About the Community

The Village at Prestonwood is a 55+ Retirement Community that offers the lock and leave convenience of a condominium in a spacious one story home – a home so specifically tailored to empty nesters that its design is patented. It is a well conceived project based on the communities originally designed and built by Epcon, Inc. Take ownership of a new lifestyle, have more free time and social interaction in this 55+ Active Adult Community.

Privacy

Outside entrance to each home has a private feel ∙ Attached two car garage permits you to come and go directly into your home ∙ Gated entrance provides controlled access.

Recreation

Sit by the pool and relax with family and friends ∙ Reserve the clubhouse for private parties ∙ Enjoy leisure walks through the grounds or up the tempo in the fitness room to maintain good health. Arbor Hills Nature Preserve is nearby at the intersection of Marsh Lane and Parker Road.

Carefree Living

Travel worry-free with no exterior maintenance and zero yard work ∙ Devote more time to pursue your passions and develop your talents.

Convenience

Close to several shopping centers, supermarkets, restaurants, and entertainment ∙ Minutes from major highways, healthcare, Arbor Hills Nature Preserve and much more…

Sense of Community

Attend neighborhood events at the clubhouse with many opportunities to meet and interact ∙ Rediscover that “friendly” small town atmosphere with neighbors who know you by name and share the same zest for life.
