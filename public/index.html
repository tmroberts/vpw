Active Adult Community

This distinctive gated community offers low-maintenance living for active adults. Every aspect of our neighborhood and one story design is considered from your perspective.

With two and three bedroom floor plans, these homes are open and spacious, featuring a light and bright sunroom with an attached two car garage. You will feel right at home amid gracious landscaping and lovely surroundings.
