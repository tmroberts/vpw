VILLAGE AT PRESTONWOOD

An Active Adult Community

About the Community

The Village at Prestonwood offers the lock and leave convenience of a condominium in a spacious one story home – a home so specifically tailored to empty nesters that its design is patented. It is a well conceived project based on the communities originally designed and built by Epcon, Inc. Take ownership of a new lifestyle, have more free time and social interaction in this 55+ active adult community.

Privacy

Outside entrance to each home has a private feel ∙ Attached two car garage permits you to come and go directly into your home ∙ Gated entrance provides controlled access

Recreation

Sit by the pool and relax with family and friends ∙ Reserve the clubhouse for private parties ∙ Enjoy leisure walks through the grounds or up the tempo in the fitness room to maintain good health

Carefree Living

Travel worry-free with no exterior maintenance and zero yard work ∙ Devote more time to pursue your passions and develop your talents

Convenience

Close to several shopping centers, supermarkets, restaurants, and entertainment ∙ Minutes from major highways, healthcare and much more…

Sense of Community

Attend neighborhood events at the clubhouse with many opportunities to meet and interact ∙ Rediscover that “friendly” small town atmosphere with neighbors who know you by name and share the same zest for life
